Travel to Malpe beach

Malpe beach, situated in Karnataka,is one of the most beautiful beaches in South India.This beach is best for water activities like paragliding,jetsking,etc

St.Mary's Island,an island situated near Malpe, has a rich ecosystem with lot of sea shells and clear water.
